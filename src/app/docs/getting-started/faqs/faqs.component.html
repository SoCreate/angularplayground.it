Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Playground redirect away from my component on reload?
Playground itself does not do any routing. If you encounter this issue, it is likely due to the `RouterModule` being provided to your sandboxed component, leading it to perform routing as it would in your app. In order to prevent this, you need to instead provide the `RouterTestingModule` in your playground configuration. ##### Do this... `RouterTestingModule.withRoutes(YOUR_ROUTES)` ##### ...not this `RouterModule.forRoot(YOUR_ROUTES)` ##### On Push Change Detection Not currently supported on the 'playground-app'
